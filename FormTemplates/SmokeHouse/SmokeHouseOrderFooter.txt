We want to thank you for your order. We will ship it on the above mentioned dispatch date. Attached please find our order confirmation. Also, you will receive automated emails by UPS informing you about the progress of the delivery once the order has left our premises. 
Please note that the smoked salmon can easily travel for 3 days without being chilled. So if it feels warm on arrival, just put it in the refrigerator, and it will be good until the end of the best before date on the back of the packs. 
Please note that the Best Before Date is stated in this format: Day/Month/Year
For deliveries to all non-EU countries (Canada, Japan etc.): 
Please allow a few more days for the processing and dispatch of the orders. Due to a change of the regulations regarding the export of smoked fish, we have to apply for a health certificate for every single order to go out, and issuing it can take up to 4 days.
There is a possibility of customs holding up delivery for shipments. Unfortunately, this is arbitrary, beyond our control and cannot be influenced. We cannot take responsibility for this.
